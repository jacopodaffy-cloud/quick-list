Delete your QuickList account

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by following the steps below.

How to delete your account

1

Open the QuickList app or visit qwicklist-v3.web.app

2

Tap the account icon in the top right corner

3

Tap Delete account, then confirm

4

Your account and all lists are permanently deleted immediately from the database

What gets deleted

All your lists, items, and account information (email address, display name) stored in our database are permanently deleted right away. Nothing is retained.

Can't access the app? Request deletion via email

In-app deletion is instant. The email option is a fallback and is processed within 30 days.
